Welcome to Redditch Smile Care

Based in the heart of Redditch, we are an independent dental practice near you established for over 50 years as the NHS and private dentist of choice for local residents.

We offer a fantastic range of fantastic General and Cosmetic dentistry which includes root canal treatment, cosmetic crowns, dentures, smile design, veneers, composite bonding, tooth whitening and tooth straightening with clear 2aligners

From the ultimate private specialist experience to much more affordable NHS dental services - we have a wide range of treatment options for all. We pride ourselves on offering a fair and transparent service irrespective of your means.

Flexible Opening Hours

We understand how hard it can be to find time to book a dental appointment, which is why we offer extended opening hours on both Tuesdays and Thursdays. We're also open every Saturday if you'd prefer to visit us on a weekend.

We are open from Monday to Saturday (except bank holidays ).

Our opening times are:

Monday – 8:30 am until 5 pm

Tuesday: 8:30 am until 7:30 pm

Wednesday – 8:30 am until 5 pm

Thursday – 8:30 am until 7:30 pm

Friday – 8: 30 am until 5 pm

Saturday – 9 am until 1 pm

For emergencies outside of normal opening hours please call 111.

Finding our practice

Our practice is easily accessible by public transport as we're a five-minute walk from the town centre, bus and train stations. If you'd prefer to drive, we have a small car park available on-site and free on street parking. Our practice has wheelchair access.
