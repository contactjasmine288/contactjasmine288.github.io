our team

Our team consists only of the best talents

Dr Jasmine Chopra

Our dental care team is expertly led by the principal dentist Dr Jasmine Chopra (GDC 241881) BDS, MFDS RCS Ed ( 2014) , PG Cert Operative Dentistry (2020)

Jasmine has over 15 years of extensive experience in Dentistry. Jasmine qualified for Faculty of Dental Surgeons Royal College of Surgeons of Edinburgh in 2014 and has completed the Post Graduate Certification in Operative Dentistry from King’s College, London in 2020. Jasmine joined the Practice in 2017 and took over as Principal Dentist of Redditch Smile Care in 2024 . Jasmine enjoys all aspects of Dentistry with special interest in Composite bonding, Root Canal Treatments and constructing cosmetic dentures. When not at work, Jasmine loves to paint on canvas and is a regular at doing Yoga.

Dr Sindhu Nair

BDS,MJDFRCS, MSc in Aesthetic Dentistry(KCL),GDC(84867)

Sindhu graduated in 2001 and has got special interest in cosmetic aspects of dentistry. Sindhu prefers treating nervous children and adults using non-sedative behaviour management techniques. Sindhu has been an invisalign provider since 2009 and offers multiple aspects of smile change including composite bonding, veneers, crowns, whitening treatments and facial aesthetics.
