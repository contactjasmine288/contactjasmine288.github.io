Dental treatments and services at Redditch Smile Care

Top quality dentistry in a modern, friendly clinic

At Redditch Smile Care, we put our patients first and always strive for the best possible outcome. We provide caring dentistry in a comfortable and friendly environment, using only the best equipment and materials available. Our surgeries utilise the latest technology including x-ray scanners.

•General dentistry:

Check-ups, hygienist appointments, fillings, tooth extractions, emergency appointments, and X-rays.

• Orthodontics:

orthodontic treatment using systems like invisalign and clear aligners.

• Restorative dentistry:

crowns, bridges, dentures, endodontics (root canal treatment), composite bonding.

• Cosmetic dentistry:

In surgery Philips ZOOM whitening Home teeth whitening kits, in-chair teeth whitening under supervision including Philips ZOOM! treatments.

If you're nervous about any dental treatment, please ask our friendly staff about possible sedation options.
